About the Project

LUCID is my capstone short film and a complete creative production project, developed from concept through post-production. The film uses suspense, cinematic imagery, and surreal visual details to create a world where reality never feels fully secure.

Logline

LUCID is a short psychological narrative film that explores the unstable line between dreams and waking life through tension, visual atmosphere, and dreamlike unease.
